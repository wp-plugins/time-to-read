== Description ==

Displays the wordcount and estimated reading time of the plugin. Based on and dedicated to <a href="http://www.nickpagan.com/blog/">Nick Pagan</a>, who adds this information manually to the beginning of each post.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `time-to-read.php` to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Put `<?php time_to_read(); ?>` in your template, perhaps between the title and content of posts.
